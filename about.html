MEET THE FOUNDER

A passionate and dynamic creative person with 10 years of experience in conceptualization and hands-on art/creative direction skills. Expertise in executing ideas into final outputs with various solutions and a keen eye for aesthetic design.

In addition to her creative pursuits, she is also interested in conservation and environmentalism. She is currently involved in various collaborative projects and channeling her concern of environmental damage through diverse Nature-based Solution (NbS) approaches, such as generating profit from live music concerts (Sofar Sounds) and donating its profit for Mangrove adoption, organizing sustainable and eco-friendly events.

She is also pursuing her passion for art and design by establishing an online platform for digital art magazines (Slevin magazine), which enabled her to connect with like-minded creatives worldwide.
